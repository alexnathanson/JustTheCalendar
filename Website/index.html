Just the Calendar (version 0.0.1) is a tool for limiting your social media presence while avoiding the fear of missing out (FOMO). It is literally just your Facebook calendar in a less social and news feed free environment.

If you are interested in becoming a test user fill out this form and you will be given access. Email admin@justthecalendar.com with questions.

If you are interested in becoming a test user email admin@justthecalendar.com.

How does its work?
The Facebook Graph API provides all of the necessary information to create Just the Calendar.

Do you store any of my data?
Nope! Just the Calendar does not store any of your data at all. All calender info that is retrieved from Facebook is only stored on your browser, until you close the window. We don't even pull your name from the API.
